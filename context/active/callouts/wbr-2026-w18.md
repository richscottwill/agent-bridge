## MX

WoW CVR did the work on both sides. Brand regs +27% on +14% clicks and +12% CVR. NB regs +15% on -9% clicks and +27% CVR, with NB CPA falling to $120 (-19%). Sparkle continues to lift Brand (+625% YoY); the NB saturation regime flagged in W16 has eased as CVR rebuilt.

Note: ie%CCP at 44% is the lowest since the Sparkle ramp; I have asked Lorena whether NB spend should step up to pull ie%CCP back toward 70%.
