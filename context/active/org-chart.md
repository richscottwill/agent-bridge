### Amazon Retail Central Paid Search (Hydra) — AME Search
The central team that manages paid search for Amazon Retail globally via the Hydra platform. AB operates within Stores, so this is the core paid search org you sit alongside. They manage ~400MM keywords/ASINs across Google, Bing, Yahoo JP. Channels: Standard Text Ads, Amazon Branded Keywords (ABK), Shopping Ads (PLAs). Bidding powered by ML bidder "Midas."
