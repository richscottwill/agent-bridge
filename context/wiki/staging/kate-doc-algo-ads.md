# Workstream 5: Algorithmic Ads

*Progression: Google Discovery Ads → Demand Gen → DG Creative & Audience Expansion → AI Max Testing*

## What We Did

The PS team proactively tested new Google campaign types beyond traditional keyword campaigns, launching Discovery Ads in 2023. While initial registrations were limited, the team continued iterating mid-funnel strategies. In 2024, we incorporated LiveRamp targeting capabilities to reach existing customer audiences in the US, improving audience precision for engagement campaigns.

### Demand Gen Performance (2025)

Demand Gen ads drove cost-efficient traffic at a fraction of keyword campaign costs:

| Metric | Demand Gen | Keyword Campaigns | Difference |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|------------|
| CPC (2025 avg) | $0.39 | $2.43 | -84% |
| CPC (Video, early 2026) | $0.30 | — | In line with image |

**So what:** DG delivers qualified traffic at 84% lower cost than keyword campaigns. This is not a volume play — it is an efficiency play that extends PS reach into mid-funnel visual placements where keyword campaigns cannot compete.

In Q4 2025, the team strategically focused on DG campaigns:

| Metric | Q4 2025 | Q4 2024 | YoY Change |
|--------|---------|---------|------------|
| Traffic | +53% | — | +53% YoY growth |
| Spend | -35% | — | -35% YoY decrease |
| CPC | -58% | — | -58% YoY improvement |

**So what:** More traffic at less cost — the efficiency gains compounded. Q4 DG delivered 53% more traffic while spending 35% less, proving the channel scales without proportional budget increases.

### Prime Day 2025

During Prime Day 2025, the Engagement campaigns drove:
- 80K clicks at -10% cost YoY
- $329K in OPS at 644% ROAS
- 12x improvement over the prior year

This validated visual ad formats as a scalable and cost-efficient channel for driving qualified traffic during high-value events.

### Business Essentials Launch

The team launched Business Essentials through DG image placements, delivering 52K visitors in the first year at a $0.30 CPC. BSE video launched in January 2026, replicating the proven DG image approach while optimizing through short video creative and increased image variations.

## 2026: Video Expansion and AI Max

### DG Video

With Demand Gen images now a proven channel, the team is expanding to video assets. Early testing shows video CPCs in line with image asset CPCs ($0.30 for both), indicating strong efficiency potential. The strategy replicates the proven DG image approach while optimizing through short video creative and increased image variations, developed in collaboration with the Creative team (Raven Smith).

### AI Max (US Test, Q2 2026)

AI Max represents Google's next evolution in campaign intelligence — expanding keyword matching beyond exact and phrase match to semantically related queries, dynamically selecting landing page experiences based on user intent, and unlocking enhanced audience signals from Google's cross-property data.

**What "expanded reach" means for AB:** AI Max will show AB ads to users searching queries that don't contain our exact keywords but signal business purchasing intent. For example, a search for "office supplies for small company" could trigger an AB ad even if that exact phrase isn't in our keyword list. This expands the addressable audience beyond what manual keyword targeting can reach.

The team is approaching this test with the same measurement discipline used for OCI:
- Clear baselines established before launch
- Phased rollout (US first)
- Incrementality benchmarks defined
- Guardrails to prevent two specific risks: (1) **cannibalization of existing keyword performance** — AI Max's expanded matching could bid on queries already covered by existing campaigns, inflating costs without incremental registrations; and (2) **budget inflation from broad matching** — without controls, expanded reach could consume budget on low-intent queries that drive clicks but not registrations
- Coordination with other Amazon teams (Hydra/Retail PS, Daniel Carmody's team) and Google (Mike Babich) to avoid internal auction overlap

AI Max requires consolidated campaign structures to function effectively — the Modern Search consolidation work directly enables this test. If the US test validates efficiency and incremental growth, the learnings will inform a broader WW rollout strategy.

**Test design due: March 28, 2026.**
