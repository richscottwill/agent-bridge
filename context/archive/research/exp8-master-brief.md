## Decision Principles (how I decide)
1. Evidence over intuition — test before scaling
2. Efficiency over escalation — smarter execution, not bigger budgets
3. Holistic measurement — total program, not Brand vs NB in isolation
4. Phased rollout — 50/50 splits over big-bang migrations
5. Cross-functional partnership — PS as connective tissue, not channel executor
6. Compression over complexity — consolidate, simplify, let algorithms learn
7. Customer research over assumptions — SP study drove ad copy overhaul
