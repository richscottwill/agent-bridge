## Market Deep Dives

### US — Strong, OCI-powered growth
- Jan: 39K regs (+30% vs OP2, +86% YoY) — peak OCI impact
- Feb: 32.9K regs (+16% vs OP2, +68% YoY) — normalizing but still well above plan
- Brand CPA pressure: $65-77 range (was ~$40 pre-Walmart)
- Walmart IS: 37-55% on Brand Core, pulling back during holidays, aggressive Jan-Mar
- Response: Bid caps on Brand, NB efficiency via OCI absorbs the CPA increase at program level

### UK — Efficiency gains despite spend reduction
- Surpassed OP2 by 24% in Feb despite -6% spend vs OP2
- Ad copy test (Jan 29-Mar 2): +86% CTR, +31% regs, +70% CTR pre/post
- Competitor: weareuncapped.com at 24% IS since Dec 2023; Amazon Global Logistics UK emerging W8-W10

### DE — Slight miss, high baseline
- Missed OP2 by 4% in Feb; NB performance -22% vs OP2
- Context: Y25 was unusually strong, making the baseline harder to beat
- OCI lift tracking: W49-W51 showed +16-20% lift, 74-96% to expectation

### CA — LP optimization paying off
- 2.8K regs, +18.5% vs OP2, +32.3% YoY
- Bulk CVR: 0.82% to 2.35% (+186.6%), Wholesale CVR: 0.75% to 2.10% (+180%)
- OCI E2E testing launched 3/4, full impact projected Jul 2026

### JP — MHLW headwind
- 1.6K regs, -47.5% vs OP2
- MHLW (Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare) campaign ended 1/31 — was a major registration driver
- OCI E2E launched 2/26; new competitors emerging on Yahoo (shop-pro.jp 12-15% IS)

### AU — Intentional efficiency trade-off
- 1.1K regs, -1% vs OP2
- Traffic decline WoW in WBR — attributed to bid strategy changes + Back to Biz ending
- Deep dive pending (requested by Harjeet/Alexis 3/11)
- New acquisition promo: 20% off, AU$50 max — link updates pending

### MX — Steady growth
- 1.1K regs, +32% vs OP2, +37% YoY
- $68K spend
- Competitor: algo-mas.mx at 11-13% IS on Brand, +20% CPC spikes
- Invoice mgmt: Jan $65K on PO MS-20200908, Dec $39K split between POs
