## Method

Conservative scan of `~/shared/context/intake/session-log.md` for mentions of each installed asset's name. Matches were filtered to lines with activation-adjacent vocabulary (activated / invoked / called / used / triggered / fired). Counts are **approximate mentions**, not true MCP-tool-level activations — the skill corpus has never been systematically activated through the `discloseContext` / `kiroPowers activate` pathway, so session-log mentions are the best pre-pilot proxy. Expect the real activation count to diverge from these numbers once the pre-draft keyword matcher (task 3.4) is wired and live.
