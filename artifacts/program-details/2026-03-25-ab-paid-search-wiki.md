## Campaign Types

| Type | Purpose | Bidding | Notes |
|------|---------|---------|-------|
| Brand | Capture branded searches | Manual CPC (bid caps) | Competitive defense vs Walmart |
| Non-Brand (NB) | Acquire new customers | OCI (where available) or manual | Primary growth driver |
| Category | Vertical-specific targeting | Manual or OCI | AU, MX category pages |
| Engagement | Lifecycle/re-engagement | Manual | F90 program |

## Key Initiatives (2026)

1. OCI Rollout — scaling to remaining markets (CA, JP, EU3)
2. Polaris Brand LP — WW rollout of new landing pages
3. Ad Copy Testing — SP study-driven messaging overhaul
4. AI Max — US test planned Q2
5. Project Baloo — Shopping Ads for AB (US Q2)
6. F90 Lifecycle — post-registration engagement
7. AEO/AI Overviews — zero-click search strategy

OCI, Polaris, and Ad Copy are in-flight. AI Max, Baloo, and F90 are planned for Q2. AEO is strategic positioning, not an active project yet.
