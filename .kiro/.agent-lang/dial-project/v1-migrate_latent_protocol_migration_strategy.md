Migrating the Swarm: Versioning Latent Space
Versioning an emergent mathematical language presents a unique challenge. When you update a traditional API, you deprecate specific text endpoints. When you update a latent protocol, the entire coordinate system of your multi-agent universe changes. A vector that meant "execute the search function" in v1 might mean "delete the database" in v2.

To migrate an active swarm without system downtime, you must treat the migration as a problem of continuous spatial translation and probabilistic routing.

Stand up a complete, independent v2 swarm (Green) in the background.Phase 2: Shadow Routing (Testing Semantics)Duplicate incoming user requests.
