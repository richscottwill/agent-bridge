# BP Engagement - Keywords

Multi-tab keyword list for Business Prime engagement campaigns. Tab 1: AB Prime keywords (exact + phrase match for amazon business prime, prime business account, etc.). Tab 2: 3PL and B2B Fulfillment keywords (3PL fulfillment partner, B2B logistics providers, competitor names like ShipMonk/ShipBob). Tab 3: Document Management and Storage keywords (DMS software, cloud document storage, competitor names like Dropbox, PandaDoc, Zoho Docs).
