# AU Paid Search — Market Overview

Last updated: 2026-04-03

---

## Overview

Australia is Amazon Business Paid Search's newest market, launched June 2025. The program runs Google Ads campaigns targeting B2B customer registrations across brand and non-brand keywords. AU operates without OCI (Offline Conversion Import) support — the only active PS market still relying on click-based bidding rather than conversion-optimized signals. OCI is targeted for May 2026.

AU sits within the ABIX (Amazon Business International Expansion) portfolio alongside MX. Richard Williams manages day-to-day campaign optimization, keyword strategy, landing page coordination, and stakeholder reporting. The market is small in absolute spend but disproportionately visible due to active scrutiny from the AU country leadership team.

## Current Performance

As of W13 (week ending ~March 30, 2026):

| Metric | Value | Trend | Context |
|--------|-------|-------|---------|
| NB Registrations | Declining | -9% WoW | Traffic-driven decline + CVR drop |
| CPA | ~$132 | Elevated | Lena requesting weekly CPA review |
| Avg CPC | ~$6 | Stable | Challenged by Lena vs Consumer benchmarks ($0.18–$0.50) |
| Landing Pages | Polaris | Migrated | Full switch from MCS completed 3/24–25 |

The CPC comparison to Consumer is an apples-to-oranges framing. B2B keywords carry inherently higher CPCs due to lower search volume, commercial intent, and no Shopping Ads availability for AB. Brandon and Richard built a data-backed response (Loop doc, 3/19) addressing this directly. Lena's follow-up questions (4/1) on landing page URLs, redirect behavior for logged-in customers, and potential CPA overstatement from repeat visitors remain open.

## Active Initiatives

**Polaris Brand LP Migration** — Full switch from legacy MCS pages to Polaris completed 3/24–25. Lena directed a full migration (no phased 50/50 test). Alexis provided keyword-to-URL mapping. Weblab for WW Polaris rollout submitted 3/21, with dial-up targeting April 6–7. AU is Brandon's top priority market for Polaris (AU > MX > DE > UK > JP).

**Keyword CPA/CPC Investigation** — Lena's top priority. Focus is on outlier analysis (top 20 keywords by CPC/CPA), not broad keyword removal. Brandon aligned on this approach (3/23). A rolling 4-week keyword CPA dashboard is in development. Biweekly keyword review cadence agreed with Alexis.

**Two-Campaign Structure Proposal** — Alexis proposed splitting into product-intent vs. business-intent campaigns (3/24). This would improve bid control and reporting granularity. Under evaluation.

**OCI Preparation** — AU OCI launch targeted for May 2026. Adobe bi-weekly syncs ongoing (Suzane Huynh). APAC MCC access issues (Google account 852-899-4580) were escalated to Google via Mike Babich and are being resolved. JP OCI is now fully operational (MCM-147368188 completed 4/1), providing a template for AU rollout.

**Paid Media CPA Challenge** — Lena cited an $80 paid media CPA for AU (4/1), which the team believes is based on view-through conversions rather than click-through attribution. Yun confirmed paid offsite shows 0 registrations in Hubble. Brandon is coordinating with Dwayne to ensure AU folds into the existing redirect plan without special treatment.
