# 2024.12.10 - IT Bid Modifiers Test
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/LJb9AAugth6

## Experiment
**Question**: Will adding bid modifiers to NB campaigns improve performance?
**Account**: Italy Google Ads
**Timeline**: 11/14 to 12/12
**Targeting**: 24 largest cities within IT

## Results
+18% Registrations, +7% CVR with city-level bid modifiers.

| Metric | City+Modifiers | Italy Only | Difference |
|---|---|---|---|
| Impressions | 32,451 | 26,731 | +21% |
| Clicks | 6,760 | 6,141 | +10% |
| Cost | $37,586 | $31,320 | +20% |
| Registrations | 383 | 324 | +18% |
| CVR | 5.70% | 5.30% | +7% |
| CPA | $98 | $97 | +2% |

Bayesian PPR: 83.4% (Amazon uses 66% threshold)
