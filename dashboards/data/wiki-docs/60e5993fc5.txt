# Ad Copy Test Results — Evidence-Based Optimization

Last updated: 2026-03-12
Sources: PS Testing: Ad Copy Updates Quip, AB Sole Prop Experience study (WorkDocs), WBR callouts

## Research Foundation
The August 2025 AB Sole Proprietor Experience study benchmarked the end-to-end CX of Sole Proprietors and identified opportunities to improve Amazon's offerings in US and UK. Key findings that drove ad copy changes:

### What SPs said matters most when choosing suppliers:
- Price: 31% US, 22% UK
- Product quality: 25% US, 33% UK
- Selection: 21% US, 15% UK

### What SPs said matters least:
- Ability to buy in bulk: 27% US, 19% UK
- Location of store: 24% US, 23% UK
- Rewards program: 22% US, 25% UK

### Why SPs without B2B accounts didn't sign up:
- Believed B2B required bulk purchasing: 50% US, 49% UK
- Savings wouldn't justify perceived costs: 31% US, 26% UK
- Business wouldn't qualify: 19% US, 18% UK

**Critical insight:** SPs believe AB is not free and requires bulk purchasing. Our existing ads reinforced both misconceptions.

## What Changed in Ad Copy
Shifted from bulk/wholesale/B2B messaging → price, quality, selection messaging.

### Headlines (examples)
| Before | After | Rationale |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Online Bulk Purchasing | Smart Business Buying | Updated brand messaging |
| Online Wholesale Purchasing | For Businesses of All Sizes | Addresses qualification concern |
| Purchase at Wholesale Price | No Minimum Order Required | Addresses bulk concern |
| Wholesale & Bulk Prices | Quantity Discounts | Reframes value without bulk framing |

### Descriptions
| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Access Millions of Business Products and Bulk Download VAT Invoices. Join Now. | Register for Amazon Business to Unlock Exclusive Pricing and Quantity Discounts. |
| Business Prices and Bulk Discounts Specifically for Businesses. Create a Free Account. | Access Millions of Quality Products with Business-Only Pricing. No Minimum Order Required. |

### Additional tactic: Keyword Insertion
- Only 6% of UK Brand ads and 23% of UK NB ads included keyword insertion in headlines
- Adding keyword insertion allows Google to match user intent more precisely
- Recommendation from Google: "include more keywords" for Good/Average ad strength ads

## Test Results

### UK AMZ Portfolio (Phase 1 — Jan 29 to Mar 2, 2026)
**Test vs Control (same period):**
| Metric | Control | Test | Difference |
|--------|---------|------|------------|
| Impressions | 8,391 | 19,586 | +133% |
| Clicks | 1,271 | 5,504 | +333% |
| Cost | $4,663 | $15,402 | +230% |
| CTR | 15.15% | 28.10% | +86% |

**Pre/Post comparison (AMZ Portfolio):**
| Metric | Before (Dec 27-Jan 28) | After (Jan 29-Mar 2) | Change |
|--------|----------------------|---------------------|--------|
| Impressions | 37,388 | 28,010 | -25% |
| Clicks | 5,308 | 6,778 | +28% |
| Cost | $19,253 | $20,072 | +4% |
| Registrations | 273 | 358 | **+31%** |
| CTR | 14% | 24% | **+70%** |
| CVR | 5.10% | 5.30% | +3% |

**Interpretation:** Despite 25% fewer impressions, the new ads drove 28% more clicks and 31% more registrations. The CTR improvement of 70% is the headline metric — the ads are significantly more compelling to the target audience.
- Confidence: HIGH (30-day test, meaningful volume)

### IT (Phase 1 — Feb 19 to Mar 5, 2026)
| Metric | Control | Test | Difference |
|--------|---------|------|------------|
| Clicks | 14,764 | 392 | -97% |
| Impressions | 127,527 | 2,944 | -98% |
| Cost | $34,029 | $544 | -98% |
| CTR | 11.58% | 13.32% | +15% |

**Interpretation:** IT test is still in very early stages with minimal test traffic. CTR directionally positive at +15%, but volume too low to draw conclusions. Need more time.
- Confidence: LOW (insufficient volume)

## Connection to Competitive Strategy
This test directly addresses the competitive pressure documented in competitor-intel.md. As competitors increase Brand CPCs, the team's response is not to escalate bids but to make ads more compelling — driving higher CTR and CVR from the same or fewer impressions. This is a sustainable competitive advantage because it's based on customer research rather than budget.
