# Polaris Brand LP Rollout Timeline

Polaris is the new brand landing page template replacing legacy PADESARJAD pages across all paid search markets. Richard is the single point of contact for global Polaris initiatives per Brandon's April 14 direction. This timeline captures market sequence, dates, owners, and dependencies in one place. It is the reference Brandon asked for and has been overdue on the action list.

## Context

The April 16 MCS decision confirmed that all paid search landing pages will migrate to Polaris branding. Legacy PADESARJAD pages are deprecated — no further investment. The strategic intent is consistency across markets, reduced MCS maintenance burden, and alignment with the broader MCS template unification roadmap. Mexico is the early test market. US and JP are already live on Polaris via weblab.

## Market sequence

Brandon's priority stack from March 23: **AU > MX > DE > UK > JP > FR > IT > ES > CA > US-ES**. The sequence reflects impact weighted by market size, existing Polaris readiness, and the Lena-challenge context in Australia. Each market follows a standard rollout pattern: Vijeth-led XF and template completion, weblab ticket submission, weblab dial-up, full switch.
