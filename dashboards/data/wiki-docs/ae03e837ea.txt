# 2025.06 JP LP Experiment
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/PbI9AAI6SDn

**Question**: Will a LP with more context about AB vs Amazon drive higher CVR than Callback LP?
**Answer**: Callback LP conclusively outperformed the blog post test page.

## R1 Results (6/12-6/30)
| LP | Cost | Regs | CVR | CPA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Control (Callback) | $418 | 7 | 4.9% | $60 |
| Test (Blog Post) | $3,412 | 32 | 2.7% | $107 |

Callback: +81.5% CVR, -44% CPA. Only 5% Bayesian probability test would outperform.

## R2 Results (7/4+)
Brand Plus: Test -38% CVR, +80% CPA
Brand Exact: Test -16% CVR, +11% CPA

**Recommendation**: Continue testing with adjusted spend/holdout. Blog post format introduces friction.
