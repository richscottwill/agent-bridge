## Key Results
- Wholesale/Bulk KW: Most successful JP NB campaign ($921 CPA) but still 10x WW avg of $85
- WTS KWs: Top JP ASINs + biz modifier in phrase match — only 1 reg from $9.4k spend
- New Ad Copy: Yahoo CTR improved (44% vs 40%), Google CTR worse (41% vs 57%)
- KW Insertion: Electronics phrase match — $3.3k CPA, not viable
- Yahoo Sitelink: Added Google sitelinks to Yahoo, drove +6 regs/week
- Discovery→DG migration: 42k more clicks but 0 regs after switching to Max Click bidding
