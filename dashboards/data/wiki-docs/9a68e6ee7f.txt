# WW Callout Review — W13 (Mar 22-28, 2026)

Reviewer: callout-reviewer (blind review)
Date: 2026-03-30
Scope: 10 markets (US, CA, UK, DE, FR, IT, ES, JP, AU, MX)

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Overall quality is strong. Most callouts are well-structured, data-grounded, and follow the principles. Three markets need attention: DE makes extensive claims about "data lag" and corrected estimates that cannot be verified from the data brief alone, making the entire narrative dependent on external context. MX states ie%CCP as "93%" in the headline, but the data brief shows 6559% for the week; this appears to be a different metric (possibly MTD pacing) that is not in the data brief. JP's YoY CPA comparison ($85 vs $73) does not match the data brief ($85 vs $73 LY for blended, but the brief says LY CPA for W13-equivalent was around $73 based on nearby weeks; however the brief explicitly states +44% regs and +67% spend, implying CPA rose, which the callout does acknowledge). The biggest systemic issue is context gaps: several markets reference external initiatives (Polaris migration, OCI, Walmart IS, AGL, MHLW, negative keyword restructuring) that are not in the data briefs, making blind verification impossible for those claims.
