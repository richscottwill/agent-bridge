# JP Paid Search Competitive Analysis
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/ETF9AASoPAb
Why NB doesn't work in JP: low brand awareness (85x less search volume vs MonotaRO), preference for in-person interaction, pricing not visible pre-registration. JP Brand CVR 50% lower than EU. MonotaRO/Askul allow product browsing pre-registration. 4 NB tests conducted 2022-2024, all unsuccessful ($410-$1,457 CPA).
