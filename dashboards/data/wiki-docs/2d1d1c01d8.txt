# 2022.10 Yahoo-JP-NB Experiment
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/OcF9AABMjFY

**Question**: Will layering audiences to Nonbrand help to reduce CPA of NB traffic?
**Account:** JP Yahoo! account
**Timeline:** October 16th to December 21st 2022

## Results
Overall: $1457 CPA.

| Audience | Impressions | Clicks | Cost | Conversions | CTR | CPC | CVR | CPA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Competitor | 330781 | 8819 | $9,691 | 9 | 2.67% | $1.10 | 0.10% | $1,077 |
| Business | 196704 | 3463 | $3,419 | 0 | 1.76% | $0.99 | 0.00% | N/A |
| Total | 527485 | 12282 | $13,110 | 9 | 2.33% | $1.07 | 0.07% | $1,457 |

Lowest CPA audience was Competitor subdomain audience (procurement.monotaro, soloel).

**Follow-up:** Overall NB didn't perform well, but Competitor audiences did relatively well. Subdomain audience had $700 CPA — half of overall. New test created for compliant terms.
