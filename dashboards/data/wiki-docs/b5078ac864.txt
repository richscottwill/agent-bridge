## G. SyRT Incrementality Data

| SyRT Arm | Market | Registration Lift | Statistical Significance | Incrementality |
|----------|--------|-------------------|--------------------------|----------------|
| Non-Brand | US | +16.4% | p<0.001 | 82-92% incremental |
| Brand | US | Directionally positive | Not statistically significant | Lower — high-intent users |
| Non-Brand | UK | Positive | Significant | Confirmed NB as primary driver |

**So what:** SyRT proved the channel's value — 82-92% of NB registrations would not have happened without Paid Search. This data justified NB budget allocation and validated the channel's existence beyond demand capture. Test period: Q2 2023. Multi-arm design to isolate Brand vs. Non-Brand effects.
