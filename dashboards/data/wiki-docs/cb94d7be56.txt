# Research Brief: PS Five-Year Outlook (2026-2030)

## Request
The wiki-editor requested a structured research brief covering all 5 years (2026-2030) of Amazon Business Paid Search trajectory. Audience: Kate Rundell (L8), Brandon Munday (L7). Type: Strategy. The wiki-writer will use this to produce a five-year outlook article that separates grounded evidence from speculation, year by year.

## Key Findings

1. **OCI is the proven engine — everything else builds on it.** +35,196 incremental registrations, $16.7MM+ OPS across US/UK/DE. 7/10 markets at 100% as of April 2026. The phased rollout methodology (E2E → 25% → 50% → 100%) is now the template for every initiative. [source: organ (eyes.md), 2026-04-02, HIGH]

2. **The channel is mid-transition from keyword-driven to audience-centric.** Campaign consolidation, OCI bidding, lifecycle engagement (F90), and AI Max testing are all steps in the same direction. The 5 workstreams compound — consolidation strengthens OCI signals, OCI enables audience bidding, Baloo creates the LP AI Max needs. [source: artifact (testing-approach-outline), 2026-04-03, HIGH]

3. **GenAI search is already measurable (~1% of Amazon WW traffic/OPS) and an internal team (ACE/Ed Banty) is building MCP integrations with ChatGPT/Anthropic/Gemini.** This moves the AEO timeline from "2028 strategic" to "2026 operational awareness." [source: artifact (genai-search-traffic), 2026-04-04, MEDIUM — single Slack source, consumer Amazon data not AB-specific]

4. **Competition is broadening (1-2 per market in 2023 → 3-5 in 2026) but the defense strategy (efficiency over escalation) is working.** Walmart is the only sustained Brand threat. OCI-powered NB efficiency absorbs Brand CPA increases at program level. [source: artifact (competitive-landscape), 2026-03-25 + DuckDB competitors table W13, HIGH]

5. **The agentic infrastructure is real but early.** Current state: context/triage/drafting are agent-assisted, execution is manual. Five-stage vision from agent-assisted → self-improving. 12-18 month window before agentic marketing becomes table stakes. [source: artifact (agentic-ps-vision), 2026-03-25, MEDIUM — vision is internal, industry timeline is estimated]

6. **AB-specific data on AI Overview impact does not exist yet.** The AEO POV identifies monitoring actions but no AB keyword data has been collected. All projections about zero-click impact on B2B are based on industry-wide consumer data. [source: artifact (aeo-ai-overviews-pov), 2026-03-25, LOW for B2B-specific claims]

7. **Registration data shows healthy but uneven performance across markets.** US W9: 8,031 regs ($82 CPA). UK: 1,465. DE: 1,364. FR: 1,098. IT: 1,335. ES: 640. CA: 689. JP: 406. AU: 256. MX: 264. JP has no NB segment in data (all Brand). AU CPA highest at $142. [source: DuckDB weekly_metrics W5-W9 2026, HIGH]

### 2030 — Horizon

#### GROUNDED (evidence exists)
- None. Pure speculation.

#### SPECULATIVE (projecting without direct evidence)
- B2B acquisition is fundamentally different from 2026. The primary "customer" for many routine purchases is an AI procurement agent, not a human buyer. Human buyers are involved only for complex, high-value, or novel purchases. [source: industry trajectory extrapolation, LOW]
- The distinction between "paid" and "organic" acquisition has blurred or disappeared. AI-mediated commerce uses a combination of structured data, reputation signals, pricing APIs, and relationship history — not keyword auctions. [source: extrapolation, LOW]
