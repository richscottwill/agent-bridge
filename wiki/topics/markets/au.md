**Lena's strategic asks**:
1. Year-long plan of testing + CPA optimization initiatives, including QBR-ready output
2. Calendar integration with AU market events (end of fiscal year May-June, summer-to-work-back transition)
3. Vertical targeting on "tradies" (tradespeople) — largest individual employment sector with good on-site selection match
4. Sydney paper product test (quoted): "our paper is priced so high that they prefer to go in store to buy paper at a more competitive price than buy it on Amazon." Wants localized Sydney-specific campaign with competitive paper ASIN
5. Business Essentials ASIN list integration — Lena's selection PM building curated list, wants paid search to drive traffic to new business-essentials landing page
6. Quarterly Google review — wants AB's central Google team to run AU B2B insights like the local Google Australia team did last year
7. Keyword/search-term insights shared upstream (quoted): "to use that to then change the way that we message more broadly."

Preferred alternative: follow Business Essentials PM's curated list rather than mining ASIN data for verticals.

Ewan (Alexis's support) proposed splitting traffic within Google ads for paper test + developing dedicated paper landing page with AU team.

- Quarterly AB Google review to be scheduled
- Sydney paper test approved for scoping
