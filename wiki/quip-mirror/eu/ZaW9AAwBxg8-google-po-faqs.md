FAQ2\) Justification: What project and/or product is this related to, and what is the goal \(how would we measure success\)? Please describe impact expected if not approved.
This PO is opened for the evergreen Microsoft Paid Search program focusing on new customer acquisition to continue in 2026. Efficiency is measured by AB iE%CCP model \(CPA/3Y CCP\). On average, Paid search counts 26% of total AB EU5 registrations. Potential delay on approving the PO will cause decline in new AB customer acquisitions.

FAQ3\) Results: What benefit will result from running this campaign or investment \(ROI, registrations, leads, awareness, etc.\)? Describe assumptions made in calculation.
The goal is to drive new Amazon Business registrations through paid search while maintaining program efficiency. For FY26, our target is to achieve XK**all tiers** registrations in [REGION] Microsoft while maintaining a 65% ie%CCP

FAQ4\) Timing: How long will this be used \(approximate months of service and/or timing of campaign\)?
This AB Microsoft {REGION] paid search PO is for 2026.
