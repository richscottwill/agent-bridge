# 2026 AU Testing
### **Overall Goal**:
Reach 2026's OP2 of 12.9K registrations with the given $2M, through testing initiatives that will focus on efficiency gains early in the year (ie%CCP will become a factor in H2).

#### Bid Strategy:
* **Phase 1**: Transition from Google Ads' click-based bid management to Adobe's bid strategy that can optimize for registrations.
Target completion: Q1 2026
 * Status: In progress.
 * Expectation: Initial volatility, optimized over 2-3 weeks. Target FY26 CPA is $140, and I would expect Adobe to allow for a 5%-15% improvement in CPA without a reduction in budget. ($119 to $133 CPA Q1)
  * Adobe bid strategies have proven themselves to be better at reducing budgets with minimal loss in registrations (MX case study below)
* **Phase 2**: In addition to considering registrations, which is current practice, we will test adding additional Adobe signals, such as Registration Start page visits, to enable the bid strategy to bid more intelligently for keywords that drive significant events.
Target completion: Q3 2026
 * Status: Q3 start.
 * Expectation: This will allow us to add more positive signals (more consistent signals for Adobe), and likely more incremental improvement. Expect a smaller increase ~5% improvement in CPA.

**Landing Page Optimization**
* **Phase 1: **Brand Landing Page Optimization. MCS brand page template showed 38bps improvement in registration in US, our most mature market.
Target completion: After Adobe bid strategies have been reliably tuned. Likely begin testing in February. (Testing in both MX and AU)
* **Phase 2: **Event support. Drive traffic to the MCS page, and add banner to our best performing page during local events to further increase CVR. (We could have our event CTRs here rather than in the ad/sitelink text)
Target completion: Q3 2026

#### Other Tests/Ongoing initiatives to consider (Ongoing)
* **Event Support**: Leverage event/promo sitelinks (Back to Business promo 1/19-2/27)
* **Image Extensions**: Implemented across all campaigns in Aug 2025, but variety would help to optimize performance. We will need to coordinate with AU team to source localized images.
* **Wasted spend: **Identify keywords/search queries that are not relevant and add as negatives in appropriate campaigns.
* **Improvement in account structure**: Make sure that search queries' paths are limited. This will allow Adobe bid strategy to have a less random flow of traffic to adjust. (Keywords/Negative keywords)
* **Location/Device/Demographics: **Add these to report on and adjust modifiers for efficiency.
* **Demand Gen \(DG\) Campaigns: **Leverage visual ad formats with AU-specific imagery. The US drove efficient traffic ($0.29 CPC vs. $2.97 for keyword campaigns) with DG campaigns. This type of traffic scales efficiently, but generally a broader audience. (Ideal for events)

#### **MX market efficiency (case study)**:
 * Moved from generalized category pages to specific pages for each category, such as Office, Food/Hosp, Janitorial, Beauty, etc. (ranged from a -23% to 52% improvement at the time of implementation, although regs too low to make strong conclusions.)
 * Ad copy tests: Worked with stakeholder to improve spanish ads, but saw little results (-2% to +1% improvement in CTR)
 * Switched from Google's max clicks to Adobe bid strategy: After 3 weeks of testing in Q1 of 2025, Nonbrand CPA was reduced by 25% PoP (-14% spend, +14% regs PoP), although the CPA was high to start. For reference, we averaged a $273 CPA in January' 2025 vs. the $103 CPA in H2 that was necessary to bring ie%CCP down to 100%.
  * Q1 vs. Q2 (before ie%CCP budget cuts): Avg. monthly cost $129K to $103K (-20%), Registrations from 492 to 511 (+4%), Monthly CPA from $264 to $201 (-24%).
  * Adobe was used to efficiently reduce spend. (Spend reduction, with slight increase in regs in MX)
**CA market \(case study\):**
* The CA market has been live since 2019, with conversion data since 2020.
* Main trends:
 * Brand CPCs have increased an average of 20% per year.
 * Brand registrations have averaged about a 53% YoY increase since 2020, but most of the increases were in the first few years, with a natural decay with maturity. (+108%, +82%, +48%, +41%, -16%)
