### 2023
#### **Wk9 (2/26 - 3/4) (Pixel update)**
* Overall: +10% Registrations WoW, +4% Cost, and -6% CPA WoW.
 * **Overall**: Registrations bounced back after the week 7 holiday.
 * **Google**: Impressions decreased 10% WoW, but CTR has increased 22%. IS decreased due to real competition from [zonexpert.co.jp][3], which had a 12% IS on our top converting Brand terms. The increase in clicks is due to the bounce back from the holidays. (Clicks returned to about the same level as week 7.
 * **Yahoo**: +10% impressions, +17% clicks, +10% cost on Yahoo. This increase in traffic is from mobile Brand campaigns, as well as bounce back from the holiday week.
* Form submits +1% WoW.
#### **Wk8 (2/19 - 2/25)**
* Overall: -24% Registrations WoW, -6% Cost, and +23% CPA WoW.
 * **Overall**: The decrease in registrations can be attributed to the holiday this past week, which decreased CVR by 20% WoW and CTR by 17%.
 * **Google**: Traffic decreased (-11% impressions and -9% clicks), and CVR dropped by 21%, which caused a decrease of 28% registrations.
 * **Yahoo**: +41% impressions, +2% clicks, +19% cost on Yahoo. This increase in traffic is from mobile Brand campaigns, which increased because I'm having Yahoo bid based on impression share rather than conversions. Because of the holiday week and the drop in CVR that comes with it, I won't be able to assess registrations from the change.
* Form submits -19% WoW, along with registrations.
![image.png][4]![image.png][5]![image.png][6]#### **Wk7 \(2/12 - 2/18\)**
* Overall: -9% Registrations WoW, +1% Cost, and +12% CPA WoW.
 * **Overall**: The decrease in registrations can be attributed to -11% CVR.
 * **Google**: Traffic decreased slightly (-4% impressions and -2% clicks), but the platform was flat across every metric WoW. When looking at 2w/2w, CPCs have increased by 13%, which has affected cost efficiency.
 * **Yahoo**: +68% impressions, +10% clicks, +40% cost on Yahoo. We saw almost double the number of Brand searches related to "receipt" or "purchase history," with just 29% of those turning into clicks, and -7% registrations. These were higher CPC terms, so the spike in these terms is what affected our Yahoo CPA. (+73% WoW for Yahoo overall)
* Form submits increased by 20% WoW.
![image.png][7]![image.png][8]![image.png][9]#### **Wk6 \(2/5 - 2/11\)**
* Overall: -6% Registrations WoW, and +7% CPA WoW.
 * **Overall**: The decrease in registrations can be attributed to -4% CVR as well as slight decrease in impressions/clicks. Relatively similar week this week compared to last week when looking at traffic levels. Something that we are seeing is a gradual increase in CPC.
 * **Google**: Traffic was relatively stable; -3% impressions/clicks, but the larger changes were an 11% increase in CPC as well as -17% registrations. (We see the same trends when looking at just Brand campaigns, but with a lower increase in CPCs with a 6% increase.) This means that CVR came down for our Branded terms.
 * **Yahoo**: Yahoo performance is a different story. Traffic was flat, but we saw a 48% decrease in cost due to CPC. One reason for this may be because we're seeing less competition. This month, only techsoudan.com, but when comparing to the second half of last month, we saw companies like techsoudan.com, justanswerjp.com, zonexpert.co.jp, and rakuten.co.jp. (All had 10% IS and about 6% overlap with our ads, but rakuten was shown above our ads 34% when they competed in the same auctions. The others were less than 1%.) Losing rakuten as a competitor this week is what likely caused drop in CPCs.
  * One note is that monotaro and mercari bid on our terms for a week, but dropped out.
* Form submits decreased by 1%.
