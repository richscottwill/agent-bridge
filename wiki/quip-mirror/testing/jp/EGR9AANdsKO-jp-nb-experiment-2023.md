# 2023.01 JP-NB Experiment Doc
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/EGR9AANdsKO

**Question**: Will the Bulk LP drive a higher CVR than the Callback LP?
**Setup:** Callback (Control) vs. Bulk (Test). Split traffic via Google Ads.
**Account:** JP Google account
**Timeline:** Jan 15th to Feb 11th 2023

## Results
LP Test: Bulk page did NOT perform better than callback page.
- Control (Callback): 759 clicks, 7 conversions (0.9%)
- Experiment (Bulk): 1333 clicks, 5 conversions (0.4%)
- Bayesian: 5.8% chance experiment would perform better

NB Overall: 8 registrations, 0.38% CVR, $1028 CPA
- Winners: "Purchase" ad set ($906 CPA, 0.4% CVR), Corporate ($1003 CPA, 0.48% CVR)
- Losers: "Bulk" ($302 cost, 0 regs), "Wholesale" ($1482 CPA)
- Only converting ad was the one created by Richard + Mao

**Recommendation:** End NB campaign. Test Discovery Ads, DSA, industry-specific approaches.
