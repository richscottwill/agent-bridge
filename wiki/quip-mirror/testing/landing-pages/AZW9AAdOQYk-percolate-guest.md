# PS LP Testing: Percolate / Guest
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/AZW9AAdOQYk

## Results Summary
| Market | Type | OPS Impact | Reg Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| US | NB Wholesale | +14% OPS | -5% Reg |
| US | Brand | +0.5% OPS | +1.5% Reg |
| CA | NB Wholesale | +20% OPS | -5% Reg |
| JP | Brand Callback | +1.3% Reg | Minimal OPS |

Percolate shows products with savings on LPs. Drives higher OPS but slightly lower regs (offset by higher customer value). Now permanent on US Brand+Wholesale, CA Wholesale, JP Bulk+Callback pages.
