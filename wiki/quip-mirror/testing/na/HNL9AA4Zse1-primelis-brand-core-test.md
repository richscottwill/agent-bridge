# Brand Core - Primelis Test
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/HNL9AA4Zse1

## Overview
Testing Primelis "Cross Brand" platform for brand cost savings on US SSR paid search.
- Referred by Audible (active user)
- $20K budget for 2-month test (funded by Chetan's team, PO CC4143)
- Timeline: November 3rd to December 29th (proposed)
- Scope: US Brand Core exact match campaigns (~$600K/month spend)

## Platform Details
- API-only access to Google Ads (no employee access needed)
- Monitors organic vs paid blended CTR at search-term level
- Gradually decreases bids when organic coverage is high (80-90% solo bid rate)
- Bi-monthly reporting cadence
- Pricing: $5K-25K/month based on brand spend

## Success Criteria
Cost savings without risk to clicks/registrations.
