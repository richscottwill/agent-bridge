# PS Testing: UK MAC Impact
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/YEG9AAoOEiO

Conclusion: No significant positive impact of MAC campaigns on UK Brand performance. MAC-active periods showed -25% impressions, -9% clicks, -10% regs vs non-active. CPA identical ($63). Seasonality analysis confirms no lift.
