# PS Testing: Ads & Ad Copy
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/ReG9AAvZFTS

Image Extensions: US NB CTR trends positive across devices despite impression shifts. CA CTR down -7.4% with -23.6% impressions.
RSA Test: Google rewrote 638 RSAs. Results: $137.68 CPA vs $145 account average. Retail benchmark: +56% impressions, +57% clicks from RSA optimization.
