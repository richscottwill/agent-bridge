# PS Testing: Incrementality Test
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/NQA9AAX2DOp

SyRT RCT across US/UK/DE. US NB: 82-92% incremental. US test caused 24.9K reg loss (3/28-5/1), offset by Q1 overperformance. UK/DE tested summer to reduce impact. Google causal analysis: +3% branded searches where NB ads present; 57% of clicks went to competitors when brand ads absent.
