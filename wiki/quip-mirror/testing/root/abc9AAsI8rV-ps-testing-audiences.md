# PS Testing: Audiences (LiveRamp)
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/abc9AAsI8rV

## LiveRamp 2023 Testing
Integrated LiveRamp for 1st party audience targeting. Tested Mar-Jun 2023, suspended due to US privacy law.

Key learnings:
- AB Registrants list: 14M users → only 1.7M active on Google (13% match rate vs expected 30-50%)
- BCIvNext: 1.25M → 140k active (11% match)
- Brand terms drove 95% of cost and 96% of BP signups
- EU CPS form-fill approach: 32x higher CVR for MQLs/SQLs vs SSR landing pages
- Start simple then go granular; landing page alignment matters
