# PS Testing: CPS
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/VDf9AA5JiIY

## Historical Summary
CPS keywords (sourcing, supplier, procurement) have low volume. EU lead form pilot is only evergreen success ($262 CPA, 1.1K MALs over 17mo). US Bombora audience test: $2,182 CPL. US LiveRamp BCIvNext: 0 CPS regs (140k audience too small). CA CPS: 0 CPS regs, $3,355 CPA for MALs.

Key insight: PS cannot effectively segment CPS vs SSR audience through keyword intent. SSR PS campaigns drive 99.7% of CPS HQs organically.
