# MBet Process Improvement
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/FZQ9AARARAN

Pain points: MBet offshore team adds lag (+2 days) and errors. 84 POs annually = 126+ hours of 0-value work. MSP/R&O disparity. PO budget accrual distorts Finance docs.

Solutions proposed: PAS (Pre-Approved Spends) via XCM alignment, reduce approver levels for OP2-approved budgets, increase offshore responsibilities, switch to SmartSheets.
