# PO Process Options - Paid Search
Reference
York/Brandon Creating email proposing options to Todd/Christine to reduce time/resource commitment to current PO process
Process Conversation Notes: [MBet Process Improvement][1]

Email Draft
Currently the PS team spends 126+ hours annually simply opening and gaining approvals for 84 POs. While the PS team values frugality, this time represents nearly 0-value work as it doesn't include back-end upkeep forecasting and managing actual spend. We aim to reduce time spent on PO creation so instead we can focus on delivering impact through our ads and ensuring accurate representation of budget utilization through high-value mechanisms (R&O, & ASP).

Options.....

* BiAnnual POs:
 * Summary: Reducing PO cadence to twice annually for each country (simplified from monthly [US] and quarterly [EU])
 * Detailed Description: POs from $5M-$50M need L10 approval. Currently we avoid exceeding the
  * moving budget b/w markets (EU)
 * Risks: Low Risk. Actual budget utilization is tracked through ASP, which will continue to serve as the source-of-truth for pacing.
* PAS:
Pre-Approved Spends (PAS), formerly known as CAR (Customer Acquisition and Retention), improves controllership while increasing approval efficiency for AB Marketing spend in COUPA. This system, integrated within the Coupa platform, introduces an innovative method of managing Purchase Order (PO) budgets and approvals.

PAS automates requisition approvals within pre-set limits after Spending and Transaction Policy (ST&P) approval. Each requisition requires concurrent approval from a designated business watcher (L6/L7) and a finance watcher (L6), aligning with Coupa's standards. This process reduces the need for individual PO approvals from senior business and finance leaders (L8/L10), enhancing PO workflow efficiency. The AB Marketing Finance team must align with the Cross-Category Marketing (XCM) Financial Planning and Analysis (FP&A) team to secure initial fiscal year approvals for WW (or starting with US as a pilot) AB Marketing budgets per ST&P guidelines. At the start of each fiscal year, we seek one-time approval for the projected $317MM worldwide AB Marketing spend from VP/SVP stakeholders via the Amazon approvals tool. After approval, the XCM Finance team will load and track annual PAS budget lines against calendar year budget totals.

With the COUPA PAS process, individual purchase requisitions will be approved using this one-time approval. One business and one finance watcher will remain on each request, as required by COUPA. New and renewed vendor contracts still require ST&P approval via the Approvals Tool.

This PAS process requires alignment with the XCM team, as the cost center CC4301, where AB budget is loaded, is owned by XCM. We need to work with XCM before setting up PAS for AB. This will significantly reduce approval waiting time while maintaining budget controllership. There are more granular aspects to address beyond aligning with XCM, such as differentiating CAR budget to various line items and teams. The initial proposal is to use the 8th segment on the COUPA PAS structure. We need to collaborate with XCM and the Coupa team to set up designated 8th segments for each team's budget, ensuring clear differentiation between team budgets.

XCM team itself has been using COUPA PAS to manage its $2B fixed marketing spends starting from FY22.
