# JP - Apple Search Ads
### **Notes:**
* Peter to alert Fernando with launch (Email)
* Explain tracking (campaign impression/page views/downloads on Apple, then registration data based on pre/post)
* $36k budget
* Initially explore 4 different tactics (Search, Search Tab, PDP, Today page)

### **Approach**:
* Start with smaller budget. (~$100/day)
* Establish baseline for spend/results across placements, and get a feel for distribution across placements.
* If reach is too small, adjust settings to open up audience targeting.
* Increase budgets to maximize results for campaigns that drive results.

### Results:
Primary: Increase in pre/post registrations attributed to App in WBR
Secondary: Increase in impressions will be first indicator (Reach), then page views/downloads (Engagement)
Follow-up: If needed, conduct incrementality test after launch by removing investment in an A/B test (Geo split or Pre-post time series to compare expected vs. actual results) and measure difference in the test group.
