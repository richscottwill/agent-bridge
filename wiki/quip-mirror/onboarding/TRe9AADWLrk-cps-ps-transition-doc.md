## Current Project Status by Regions
* US
 * Currently, Commercial ([State of Procurement 2024 General][16]), Local Government ([State of Procurement 2024 Government][17]) and Education ([State of Procurement 2024 Education][18]) campaigns are active with State of Procurement landing pages personalized to each vertical. New keyword segments that are also live include Healthcare and Nonprofit keywords which identified by our team using Google keyword tools. These were launched on 8/20. Due to the CPS team not making FH, we are using the [general State of Procurement LP][19]. Any form fills generated from these campaigns will be routed to the [Commercial child campaign][20] within SFDC.
 * All legacy campaigns have been paused. **Only the 5 verticals listed above are running.**
 * Some keywords, such as “government contracts”, produced high traffic, but the broad search intent resulted in a failure to drive conversions. We look to scale these findings by sharing top traffic-driving keywords with the SEO team. This collaboration will inform their Free Search content strategy and involve reallocating high-cost non-brand keywords across channels to optimize performance. For the Paid Search high traffics keywords that aren’t aligned with the current SEO content plan, we have already begun pausing high-cost keywords to improve efficiency, such as “government public contracts” and “government bidding”. A monthly meeting has been established for knowledge sharing, and the SEO team will include the Paid Search team for further collaboration closely with the new agency as content is being planned for 2025.
 * Currently, we are collaborating with the CPS team to create a paid search form handler for Gated sector-specific landing pages (e.g., the [2024 State of Procurement report for Local Government sector][17]). The paid search-specific form handlers will enable us to track the user's journey beyond the initial submission. This will allow us to evaluate whether the leads meet the CPS lead qualification criteria and if they ultimately convert into CPS registrations. Previously, Paid Search has never been able to assess the impact of lead generation beyond the user's first interaction. This capability will be critical for future testing and performance measurement.

## Optimization Strategy
* Max Clicks bidding
* Optimization tactics:
 * Pause high spending, non-converting keywords to allow budget to other keywords
 * Negate irrelevant or poor performing search queries
 * Build out strong performing search queries into keywords
 * Add budget to high performing campaigns
 * Reduce budget on poor performing campaigns
 * Test new LPs
