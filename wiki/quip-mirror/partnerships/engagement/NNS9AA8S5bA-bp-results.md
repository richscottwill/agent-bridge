# BP Results
Source: https://quip-amazon.com/NNS9AA8S5bA
Spent $16K (24% of $67K budget). $512 CPA per BP signup, 0.3% CVR. Brand campaigns best at $373-436 CPA. AB Brand terms drove 95% of cost and 96% of signups. Campaigns paused due to CCBA privacy restrictions on 1P audience targeting.
