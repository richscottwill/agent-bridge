# US — W16 (Apr 13 – Apr 19)

US: US drove 9,109 registrations (-3% WoW), with flat spend WoW and $75 CPA (+3% WoW). April is projected at $2.89M spend and 38.8K registrations at $74 CPA. (vs. OP2: +25% regs, +2% spend)

WoW we held spend flat and let composition do the work. Brand hit 12-week highs on every dimension: regs +16%, CVR +13% to 4.64%, CPA dropping to $61. NB gave back the W14-W15 CVR step-up, softening to 5.89% on flat clicks, which lifted NB CPA to $81 and drove the -3% headline.

YoY we spent +28% with regs +92%, and CPA is down -33%. NB is the structural story: regs +135% at $81 CPA vs $143 LY.

Note: The W14-W15 NB CVR peak was not a new baseline. I'll diagnose NB at the campaign level before W17 to see whether the soften stabilizes at 5.9% or drifts further.
