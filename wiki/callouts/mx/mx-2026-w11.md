# MX — W11 (Mar 8–14)

MX drove 397 registrations (+33% WoW), with +10% spend WoW, and 83% ie%CCP. CPA came in at $57 (-18% WoW). March is projected to end at $93K spend and 1.4K registrations. (vs. OP2: +65% spend, +66% registrations)

WoW registrations increased on both Brand (+26%) and NB (+48%), driven by CVR increases on both sides (+29% Brand, +40% NB). We didn't make material budget changes, but added negative keywords across campaigns to improve search query routing between Brand Exact, Brand Phrase, and NB campaign keywords.

YoY we spent -21% with +101% registrations. All of the increase was on the Brand side, driven by increased Brand coverage and +29% CVR YoY. NB CPA improved -47% YoY ($121 vs $228) on +20% registrations despite -37% spend, enabled by -27% CPC and continued bid strategy efficiency gains.

Note: W11 NB CVR of 1.57% is above normal range of 1.1-1.3% for the past 10 weeks. May be partially due to negative keyword additions that improved query routing across campaigns. W11 WoW last year was -5% regs, and W10 was more in line with the recent trend.
