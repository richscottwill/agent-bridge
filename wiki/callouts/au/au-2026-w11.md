# AU — W11 (Mar 8–14)

AU drove 234 registrations (-7% WoW), with -2% spend WoW. CPA increased to $132 (+5% WoW). March is projected to end at $139K spend and 1.1K registrations, and $129 CPA. (vs. OP2: -6% spend, flat registrations)

WoW performance has been stabilizing since the end of the site-wide promotion in W8, as we continue to adjust bid strategies. On the NB side, registrations fell -9% WoW even as clicks (+5%) and impressions (+8%) increased. The bid strategies continued to decrease CPC (-4% WoW), now down 19% from Week 7, but the additional traffic may be coming in at lower intent as we move off the promo-driven audience. NB CPA rose to $206 (+9% WoW). On the Brand side, registrations decreased -5% WoW with -10% spend, and CPA improved -6% WoW ($43 vs $46), so the core Brand terms became more efficient WoW.

Note: I'm monitoring whether the new acquisition promo (20% off first order, up to AU$50) drives recovery in W12+. The sitelink has been updated, and we are also working with Alexis on updating remaining campaign URLs to the new Polaris pages.
