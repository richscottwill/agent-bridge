# CA W12 Analysis Brief

## Registration summary
698 regs, -7% WoW. Spend $53K (+1% WoW). CPA $76 (+9% WoW). Brand 468 (-11% WoW). NB 230 (+2% WoW). Overall CVR 2.63% (-6% WoW). March projection: 2,950 regs at $228K spend ($77 CPA) vs OP2 2,592 regs at $228K ($88 CPA). +14% regs, flat spend vs OP2.

## Why registrations changed
CVR-driven decline. CVR fell -6% WoW (2.63% vs 2.79%) while clicks were only -2%. The CVR drop did the damage.

The entire WoW decline is Brand:
- Brand regs -11% WoW (468 vs 527). Both CVR (-5%, 3.51% vs 3.71%) and clicks (-6%, 13.3K vs 14.2K) declined. Brand CPA jumped to $54 from $48 (+12%). This is a full reversal of the W11 Brand surge.
- NB regs +2% WoW (230 vs 226). NB clicks grew +4% while CVR was essentially flat (-2%). NB held steady.

W11's Brand spike (527 regs, highest in 8 weeks) was an outlier. W12's 468 is closer to the W5-W9 Brand average (~457). This is reversion, not decline.

## Trend context
W12 fits a sawtooth pattern:
- W5-W6: ~740 regs, strong
- W7-W10: gradual decline to 644 (W10 trough)
- W11: sharp bounce to 753 (Brand-driven)
- W12: pullback to 698

NB has been remarkably stable: 226-300 range for 12 weeks, averaging ~245. No trend, no volatility. NB is a steady-state contributor.

CPA has oscillated without direction: $68 (W5) → $78 (W10) → $69 (W11) → $76 (W12). CVR similarly: 2.86% (W5) → 2.39% (W10) → 2.79% (W11) → 2.63% (W12). CA is range-bound, waiting for OCI E2E to mature and shift the efficiency curve.

NB CPC has declined steadily: $2.43 (W1) → $2.09 (W12). This 14% CPC compression over 12 weeks is a quiet positive signal, likely reflecting improved keyword targeting and bid management.

## Relevant actions and events
- OCI E2E launched 3/4. Only ~17 days into E2E as of W12 end. Too early for meaningful OCI impact on NB. Full impact projected Jul 2026. Any NB movement now is organic, not OCI-driven.
- LP optimization (Bulk CVR +187%, Wholesale CVR +180%) is already baked into the current CVR baseline and explains the YoY NB CPA compression ($121 vs $224 LY, -46%).
- Shopify remains a recurring Brand competitor at +13% CPC. The -6% Brand click decline could reflect Shopify auction pressure, though W12-specific IS data isn't available.
- W11's Brand spike (527 regs) was a one-week CVR anomaly. W12's 468 is in line with the W5-W9 average (~457). Don't over-read the "decline."

## YoY assessment
+53% regs YoY (698 vs 455). Spend +15% ($53K vs $46K).
- NB: +69% YoY. NB CPA dropped 46% ($121 vs $224). This is LP optimization impact, not OCI (too early).
- Brand: +47% YoY. Solid growth despite Shopify competition.
- WoW pattern: TY -7% vs LY -11%. Both years saw a W12 dip, but TY's is shallower. Seasonal W12 softness is normal in CA; TY is performing relatively better.

LY W13-W16 showed 373-469 regs at $106-127 CPA. TY is running at 1.5x LY volume at 0.7x LY CPA. LP optimization is the structural driver.

## Monthly projection
21/31 days elapsed. MTD: 2,095 regs, $155K spend.

Remaining 10 days (Mar 22-31): 6 weekdays, 4 weekend days. No CA holidays.

Daily rate estimation from W12 actuals:
- Weekday avg: (123 + 106 + 117 + 103 + 102) / 5 = 110 regs/day, $7.8K spend/day
- Weekend avg: (74 + 73) / 2 = 74 regs/day, $7.0K spend/day

Remaining estimate: (6 × 110) + (4 × 74) = 660 + 296 = 956 regs, (6 × $7.8K) + (4 × $7.0K) = $74.8K spend.

Adjustment: LY W13 was -11% WoW from W12 (455 → 459 LY, actually +1%). No strong seasonal drag expected. Apply -5% conservatism for Brand volatility (W12 Brand was already soft, so limited further downside).

Adjusted remaining: 956 × 0.95 = 908 regs, ~$73K spend.

**Analyst projection: 3,000 regs at $228K spend, $76 CPA.**
vs OP2: +16% regs, flat spend.

## Flags
- Brand volatility: 411 → 527 → 468 over three weeks. Brand is swinging ±12% WoW. The 4-week rolling average (~463) is the true signal, not any single week.
- NB CPA at $121 is expensive. Despite -46% YoY improvement, NB CPA is still 2.2x Brand CPA ($54). OCI E2E is the fix, but full impact is months away.
- CVR ceiling: overall CVR has not broken above 2.86% (W5) in 12 weeks. LP gains may be fully absorbed. Next CVR step-change depends on OCI maturation.
- ie%CCP at 50% (up from 44% LW). Improving but still well below 100% target. Budget is not the constraint; efficiency is.

## Suggested narrative angle
W11 Brand spike reverted. W12 decline is Brand-driven (-11% WoW) while NB held flat. This is noise, not signal. Brand regs at 468 are in line with the 8-week average. The real story is the monthly projection: +16% regs vs OP2 at matched spend, powered by LP optimization. OCI E2E is 17 days in with full impact projected Jul 2026.
