# EU5 — W16 (Apr 13 – Apr 19)

EU5: EU5 drove 5,980 registrations (+18% WoW), with +31% spend WoW and $88 CPA (+11% WoW). April is projected at $1.8M spend and 22.8K registrations. (vs. OP2: +7% registrations, +7% spend)

Post-Easter recovery arrived, but we paid for it. DE drove the spend surge, jumping to $199K (+76% WoW) as NB CPA ran away to $210 (+61% WoW). UK led on volume (+15% regs) but ran hot, pushing CPA to $75 on rising NB CPCs. The rest of EU5 recovered in line with the post-Easter pattern without fully restoring pre-Easter efficiency.

YoY we spent +52% with +50% registrations and CPA +2%. UK subsidizes the group at -11% CPA YoY on OCI maturity, while the rest remains inflated through OCI E2E learning.

Note: I'm reviewing DE bid strategies and budget settings ahead of W17 to keep NB CPA from running hot a second week.
