# UK W13 Analysis Brief

## Registration summary
1,531 regs (+14% WoW), $86K spend (-5% WoW), $56 CPA (-17% WoW). Brand 577 (+31% WoW), NB 954 (+6% WoW). Projected: 6,300 regs, $396K spend vs OP2 5,174 regs, $461K spend (+22% regs, -14% spend).

## Why registrations changed
CVR drove the recovery more than clicks. CVR surged +17% WoW to 4.28%, a 12-week high, while clicks dipped -2%. Brand rebounded +31% WoW after W12's 8-week low (442), with CVR jumping +29% (3.37% vs 2.62%) on flat clicks (+1%). NB grew +6% on +12% CVR (5.12% vs 4.58%) despite -5% clicks. The CVR expansion across both segments is the story: 4.28% blended CVR is the highest since at least W2. Spend fell -5% WoW while regs grew +14%, producing the best CPA ($56) in the 12-week window.

## Trend context
UK has been in a 1,325-1,531 range for 9 weeks. W13 at 1,531 is the new 8-week high, breaking above the prior ceiling of 1,467 (W6). Brand's +31% WoW bounce from 442 to 577 reverses the multi-week erosion flagged in W12 (604 in W5 to 442 in W12). The 577 level is back within the W5-W11 range (506-605), suggesting W12's 442 was a trough rather than a new baseline. NB at 954 is also an 8-week high, extending the NB growth trend (720 in W5 to 954 in W13). The NB mix has eased slightly to 62% from W12's 67%.

## Relevant actions and events
1. No new change log entries for W13. The ad copy test gains (launched Mar 6) are fully baked in. The 3/16 budget adjustments (GA Top $2.2K to $2.5K, PV $1.8K to $2K, Brand Plus $1.2K to $1K) are now in their second week.
2. weareuncapped.com at 24% Brand IS, unchanged. Despite this, Brand CVR recovered +29% WoW. The W12 Brand trough may have been a one-week anomaly rather than competitive erosion.
3. OCI live at 100% since Sep 2025. NB CPC fell to $2.74, the lowest in the 12-week window. NB CVR at 5.12% is also a 12-week high. OCI efficiency continues to compound.
4. Cross-market context: WW +4.3% WoW. Most EU5 markets recovered from W12 mid-March softness. UK's +14% is the strongest EU5 recovery.

## YoY assessment
+117% YoY regs (1,531 vs 704). +82% YoY spend ($86K vs $47K). Brand +26% YoY, NB +286% YoY. NB CPA improved -24% YoY ($54 vs $71). LY W13 was -1% WoW; TY's +14% is dramatically stronger. The YoY gap continues to widen as OCI maturity and ad copy improvements compound against a weak 2025 baseline.

## Monthly projection
Projected 6,300 regs vs OP2 5,174 (+22%). Projected $396K spend vs OP2 $461K (-14%). Remaining 3 days: 2 weekdays, 1 weekend day. W13 daily rates: weekday ~227, weekend ~199. Remaining: 2(227) + 1(199) = 653. MTD 5,626 + 653 = 6,279, rounded to 6,300. Spend: MTD $359K + ~$37K remaining = $396K. CPA: $63. UK is the standout EU5 efficiency story: +22% regs on -14% spend, with CPA at $63 vs implied OP2 CPA of $89.

## Flags
- CVR spike: 4.28% blended CVR is a 12-week high. This level may not sustain. W14 will reveal whether this is a new baseline or a one-week peak.
- Brand recovery: 577 is a strong bounce from 442, but still below the W5-W6 level (578-605). weareuncapped.com at 24% IS remains a structural headwind.
- NB CPC: $2.74 is the lowest in 12 weeks. If this holds, NB efficiency is entering a new tier. If it bounces back, W13's CPA improvement partially reverses.

CPA dropped to $56 (-17% WoW), the best in the 12-week window. Monthly projection: +22% regs vs OP2 on -14% spend. The efficiency compounding from OCI + ad copy is accelerating.
