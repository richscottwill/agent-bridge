# Paid Search Testing Approach & Year Ahead — Outline

*For Kate Rundell | OP1 Planning*

---

## Executive Summary

- The PS team built a repeatable testing methodology that delivered +35,196 incremental registrations and $16.7MM+ in OPS across US, UK, and DE in 2025
- Five compounding workstreams — each validated through controlled testing — are now ready to scale worldwide across 10 markets
- Every 2026 investment maps directly to a validated 2025 result; nothing proposed is speculative
- The workstreams compound: consolidation strengthens OCI signals, OCI enables smarter audience bidding, and Baloo creates the landing experience AI Max requires
- Core transformation: Paid Search is shifting from keyword-driven acquisition to an automated, audience-centric engine grounded in evidence

---

## How We Test (Methodology)

- Every test follows four stages: hypothesis and baseline → phased rollout → measurement framework → scale or stop
- Baselines use prior-quarter data with seasonality adjustments to isolate the effect of the change from background noise
- No big-bang changes — OCI launched at keyword level, scaled to 25%, 50%, then 100% of campaigns with go/no-go gates at each phase
- Measurement matched to test type: seasonality-adjusted baselines (OCI), test/control splits (ad copy), pre/post causal analysis (landing pages), Weblab APT (structural UX), Bayesian PPR (bid modifiers)
- Failures are data: Gated Guest showed -61% regs → paused → pivoted to in-context registration → +13.6K annualized incremental regs at 100% probability (APT)

## Workstream 1: Intelligent Bidding (OCI)

- **Problem:** Loss of Google Ads auto-bid strategy and Adobe deprecation left the team without a scalable bidding optimization path; manual bidding could not keep pace with competitive pressure (Walmart Business driving Brand CPA from ~$40 to $65-77)
- **Test:** Partnered with MarTech, Legal, and Data Science to implement OCI as the first non-retail BU at Amazon — phased rollout from keyword-level E2E testing through 25/50/100% campaign application, with seasonality-adjusted CPA baselines at each gate
- **Result:** US +24% reg uplift, ~50% NB CPA improvement, +32,047 regs, $16.7MM OPS; UK +23% lift (+2,400 regs); DE +18% lift (+749 regs) — totaling +35,196 incremental regs; US drove +86% YoY regs
- **Scale:** Expanding to CA, JP, FR, IT, ES — tech-readiness complete Q1 2026, full monthly registration impact expected by July; DE privacy learnings (DMA restrictions) informing longer stabilization windows for EU4

---

## Workstream 2: Modern Search

- **Problem:** Fragmented device-specific campaigns and keyword themes weakened data signals for OCI; existing ad copy reinforced misconceptions — 50% of Sole Proprietors believed AB required bulk purchasing and was not free (AB Sole Proprietor Experience study, Aug 2025)
- **Test:** Consolidated campaigns to strengthen data density; shifted ad copy from bulk/wholesale/B2B messaging to price, quality, selection based on customer research — ran test/control splits in UK
- **Result:** UK Phase 1 (Jan 29–Mar 2): +86% CTR (test vs. control), +31% registrations, +28% clicks — despite 25% fewer impressions; confidence HIGH
- **Scale:** EU4 translations completed via GlobalLink; phased rollout across all non-brand campaigns worldwide; campaign consolidation continuing as structural prep for AI Max testing (US Q2 2026)

---

## Workstream 3: Audiences & Lifecycle

- **Problem:** PS operated as a pure acquisition channel with no post-registration engagement capability; Google's B2B customer match rate was only 13%, limiting audience targeting precision
- **Test:** Built dedicated Engagement account; partnered with ABMA to integrate Associated Accounts, boosting match rate from 13% to 30%; ran LiveRamp suppression and engagement campaigns
- **Result:** Engagement channel drove $765K in iOPS in 2025; Prime Day 2025 Engagement campaigns generated $329K OPS at 644% ROAS — 12x improvement over prior year
- **Scale:** F90 Lifecycle Engagement — targeting recently acquired, non-purchasing customers to complete 3+ purchases; goal: increase non-SHuMA 3+ purchase rate from 31.7% to 35.4% (+366 bps); extends PS role from acquisition into activation

---

## Workstream 4: User Experience

- **Problem:** 85% of paid search customers dropped off at the MCS landing page before starting registration — the funnel was broken at the point of highest intent
- **Test:** Ran Gated Guest experiment (failed: -61% regs) → paused and pivoted to in-context registration via Weblab APT; in CA, tested mobile-first LP optimizations on Bulk page
- **Result:** In-context registration delivered +13.6K annualized incremental regs at 100% probability (APT); CA mobile-first LP drove Bulk CVR from 0.82% to 2.35% (+187%)
- **Scale:** Project Baloo — unauthenticated subdomain allowing catalog exploration before registration, unlocking Shopping Ads for AB (US Q2 2026); EU5 LP framework from CA methodology; Project Aladdin (registration + checkout merge, Q4)

---

## Workstream 5: Algorithmic Ads

- **Problem:** Keyword campaigns alone cannot efficiently reach mid-funnel audiences; CPC for keyword campaigns averaged $2.43, limiting scalable traffic growth
- **Test:** Launched Demand Gen ads with image and video creative; applied same measurement discipline as OCI — clear baselines, phased rollout, incrementality benchmarks, cannibalization guardrails
- **Result:** DG CPC at $0.39 vs. $2.43 keyword (-84%); Q4 2025 DG achieved +53% YoY traffic growth despite -35% YoY spend decrease; bid strategies reduced NB CPC 29% in AU over 7 weeks
- **Scale:** DG Video expansion (early CPCs at $0.30, in line with image); AI Max US test planned Q2 2026 — Google's next evolution in campaign intelligence; BSE scaling across markets

---

## Compounding Effects

- Campaign consolidation (Modern Search) strengthens data signals that OCI bidding requires to optimize
- OCI enables smarter bidding on the audiences the Engagement channel reaches (Audiences & Lifecycle)
- Baloo (UX) creates the friction-free landing experience that AI Max's dynamic page selection requires (Algorithmic Ads)
- Each workstream's output becomes another workstream's input — the system compounds rather than operates in parallel
- Risk of not investing: OCI-eligible markets remain on deprecated Adobe bidding, Shopping Ads stay unavailable for AB, and the Engagement infrastructure built in 2025 sits underutilized
